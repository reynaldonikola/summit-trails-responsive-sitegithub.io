Why this site?

This project demonstrates cleaner structure, improved spacing, better alignment, and a more cohesive visual system than a basic starter page.

What changed?

The page uses organized CSS, responsive layout patterns, reusable card styling, and a full alternate theme loaded with JavaScript.

Theme toggle

The button in the header appends a separate stylesheet to the bottom of the head tag and toggles between the original and alternate theme.

Trail advice

Quick hiking tips

Start early, wear the right shoes, check conditions, and always pack more water than you think you need.

Check weather

Mountain weather changes quickly, so layer up and plan ahead.

Bring essentials

Water, snacks, and a charged phone should always be in your pack.

Know your route

Review distance, elevation, and trail conditions before you leave.
